SLEEPLESS HACKATHON

FreeLancing

Collectivised growth through Institute Freelancing accounts. Whenever the project is assigned to the accounts, desired students will be selected based on their skill set. They will be provided certificates and incentives from the college.

Alumni Alerts

As most of our Alumni are well settled in some companies, they will be the first to get notified about the potential opportunities available in their respective companies. They will Alert the students through this platform.

Work with Professors

Due to research culture in IIT's, almost all the professors will be working on some research projects and they will be needing students to work with them, so this platform will act as a bridge between professors and interested students.

Club Recruitments

Clubs make the major part of student life in IIT's. They do a lot of projects. This Platform will support both students and clubs in their journey.

Career opportunities

From now on we will even provide solutions to small startups through our students. This will provide some good industrial experience to the students and also increase institute reputation.
